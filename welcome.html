Smart DT Project helps you apply Design Thinking to solve problems and make a difference.

Your 5-Phase DT Journey

Empathize — Listen to real users. Understand their world before you solve.

Define — Frame the real problem. Write one clear problem statement.

Ideate — Generate 20+ ideas. No judging allowed yet.

Prototype — Build a rough version. Rough is good.

Test — Watch real users. Listen. Improve.

Why Design Thinking for FYP?

User-centred — Your solution solves a problem real people actually have.

Structured — Five clear phases guide you from research to solution.

Iterative — You test and improve. One version is never enough.

Portfolio-ready — Every template builds your FYP evidence portfolio.
